Coir Products

The Golden Fibre of Nature – Coir! Coir is one of earth’s greatest gift to mankind. It has an enchanting shine, robust texture and golden shade, the applications of coir in today’s world is extensive and magical. Coir is extracted from the husk of nuts of Coconut palms. And one of the most promising aspects of coir for the future generations is that its 100% organic and bio-degradable making it one of the most useful eco-friendly products available in the market. Coir has numerous uses, it can be used as coir yarn specifically for Monsoon Shed Builders, Construction builders. Coir can also be used in making ranges of Mats, Carpets, Mattings, Rugs etc. The Application of Coir are unlimited and the opportunities seems endless!

Various Coir Products

The product store will introduce you to the world of various products made with natural Indian Coir Fibre ranging from the most common Coir Yarn, Coir Mats to the special purpose of Coir Geo Textiles.

Coir Yarn

Coir Yarn and Coir Rope is literary made from coir fibre which is generated from the outer skin of coconut. Coir fibre is processed and then with the traditional skill its been made into the form of rope. Coir rope/ Coir Yarn can be machine made and Hand made too. For best results and quality, experts prefer hand made coir yarn, since its made with accuracy by individual giving it a perfect texture. Coir Yarn/ Coir rope comes is different quality and grades depending upon the quality of Fibre being used. Coir Yarn & Coir rope is mainly used for mattings, agriculture, packing & horticulture purpose, Truck, Geo Textiles etc. its also widely used by Monsoon shed builders and construction owners. Coir Yarn is available in different quality, customised length, thickness, at competitive market prices. Our Clients can select the weight ranges as per their requirement. Any Specific types can be supplied based on Client’s requirements.

Coir Pith

Coir Pith is a dust like material which is produced during the process of extracting fibre from the husk of coconuts. Coir Pith possess a unique quality of holding water. Its widely used as soil conditioner and as a growing medium in soil -less farming, it makes the soil more porous. It improves soil texture and structure, soil aggregation and water holding capacity of soil.

Coir Mattings / Carpets

Coir Products have gone through great modernization in terms of design and processing, coir mattings and Coir Carpets are woven so elegantly by the professional craftsmen so naturally that it looks very delightful to the viewers. Its Eco Friendly, bio degradable and made out of 100% organic coir fibre. Coir Mattings and Carpets are long lasting and widely used in corporate offices and villas as floor coverings, carpeting, wall and roof panelling. Its also widely used in industrial environments since it acts as a liquid absorber and its resistant to salt water has made it the best choice for pavements mats in beaches.

Coco Log

Coco Log is a coir fibre thickly filled inside tubed shaped coir netting, its shaped like a wooden log. The diameter of the logs vary from 15 to 50cm and available in the lengths of 2 to 6 m. Coco Logs are mainly used for absorbing the impact of waves in erosion prone areas. Coco Logs offer an alternate solution for embankment protection and is available in standard diameters.

Coir Tiles & Rugs

Coir Tiles offers a unique way of transforming your interiors and enhancing its ambience. Its superior quality mattings are rubber moulded so that it provides skid protection and dust holding. It has numerous colour and patterns and its 100 % Natural and Bio Degradable. Coir Rugs are best natural carpet available with its dense pile either pattern woven or printed in exquisite colour. They are available in vibrant colours and patterns.
