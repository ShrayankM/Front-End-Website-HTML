Pepper

Pepper is the oldest and the best known spice in the world, Indigenous to India its savoury flourished available in Black and White. A Parennial Vine’s fruit, which is usually sun dried and changes its color to black. Its then peeled and dried to make white pepper. Its highly Aromatic and Hot. Pepper is the vital ingredient in many Indian and foreign cuisine. Pepper not only is the gives flavour to the cuisines but its also considered to be the best in terms of digestion, consisting of medicinal properties too. Cardamom

Cardamom, famously known as Elichi – the King ingredient in Masala Tea, is the world’s most ancient spices. Highly productive in the western ghats of south India, Cardamom is used for two herbs related to ginger. Their seed are used as Spice. Its Antioxidant and its diuretic properties may help to lower the blood pressure. It Contains Cancer-Fighting compounds, also helps in digestion and treats infections. Ginger

A flowering plant whose ginger root used as a spice, is widely used in India from ancient times, It’s the earliest of all spices introduced in Europe, Ginger is highly beneficial in relieving nausea, loss of appetite, and pain. The root of the ginger plant can be consumed fresh, powdered, dried as a spice, in oil form or as juice. Ginger Spice is freshly dried and powdered.
